WHAT'S SQUISHY WORLD MISSION?
The goal of Squishy World is to provide a hug to organizations dedicated to the conservation of species around the world. We also want the community to get involved and participate in the decision-making of future collections. So we can choose between species or organizations that need help.
WHEN IS THE LAUNCH?
The launch date is right around the corner, we are looking to launch in early-mid February.
HOW DO I GET INVOLVED?
Follow us on Twitter or attempt to join our Discord. You can also email us at info@squishyworld.com
IS SQUISHY WORLD A GOOD INVESTMENT?
This is a decision you will have to make yourself. But one thing is for sure, for every squishy you collect you will be giving a hug to an animal in the world.
